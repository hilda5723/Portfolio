About Me

Discover my journey, skills, and passion for creating exceptional experiences.

Hello! I'm Hilda.

I'm a dedicated and results-oriented professional with a robust background in customer service and virtual assistance, primarily within dynamic e-commerce and digital service landscapes. My experience at Teleperformance Kenya and the Institute of Certified Studies has equipped me with the skills to expertly handle customer inquiries across multiple channels – email, live chat, and phone – always striving to resolve issues efficiently and maintain high satisfaction scores.

I'm adept with industry-standard platforms like Zendesk, Shopify, and Gorgias, which allow me to manage support tickets effectively and ensure every customer interaction is both professional and positive. My core strengths lie in my problem-solving abilities, clear communication, and an unwavering commitment to delivering top-tier customer experiences.

My Journey into Technology

Foundation in Communication

My Bachelor of Science in Communication and Public Relations from the University of Kabianga (2017-2022) provided me with a strong foundation in understanding user needs and conveying information effectively – skills that are invaluable in both customer service and software development.

Excellence in Customer Advocacy

Through roles at Teleperformance and ICS, I honed my ability to empathize with users, troubleshoot complex issues, and advocate for customer needs. This experience has given me a user-first perspective that I bring to my technical pursuits.

Embracing Software Engineering

Complementing my customer-centric expertise, I am now a budding Software Engineer, actively building proficiency in Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I am particularly excited about specializing in the MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js). My recent Virtual Assistant training from ALX Kenya and the Digital Customer Service Programme from Generation Programme Kenya further bridge my diverse skill set.

I believe that my unique blend of communication skills, customer empathy, and growing technical knowledge allows me to approach software development with a holistic understanding of both user needs and technical implementation.
